About Me

Hello! I'm Achyut Pandey, a 20-year-old passionate educator currently pursuing my studies at MSIT Delhi. As an alumnus of The Air Force School, Subroto Park, I bring a strong academic foundation to my teaching.

My journey in education has been marked by notable achievements, including being a regional topper in the Science Olympiad and securing the runner-up position in the school's Mathematics Olympiad. These experiences have not only honed my subject expertise but also given me valuable insights into competitive exam preparation.

At Kusum Tuition Classes, I combine my academic excellence with a genuine passion for teaching. I believe in creating an engaging learning environment where students can develop a deep understanding of concepts while building confidence in their abilities.
